Projects

Over the course of my term at eSight I worked on several distinct but related projects. All of them are focusing on the eStreamer app. It's basic purpose is to allow streaming from an android device to the eSight Eyewear device. When I started work on the app it was very basic, but by the end of my term it had the following features:

  • Stream the eSight camera to the Android Devices
  • Stream the Android Device's camera to the eSight Display
  • Cast the Android Device's screen to the eSight Display
  • Pair an Android Device with the eSight Device
  • Stream over wifi OR Bluetooth.
  • iOS and Mac OS X support.
  • Remote Control of the eSight Device from the Android phone.
